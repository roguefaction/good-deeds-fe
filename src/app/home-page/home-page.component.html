Good deeds is a project to help bring people together and to do good deeds. . The things we do are the most important things of all. They are more important than what we say or what we look like. The things we do outlast our mortality.

Good deeds can be done at any place, by anyone willing to do them. If our world will be full of bad deeds of all kind, you can imagine the kind of world we shall be living in!
